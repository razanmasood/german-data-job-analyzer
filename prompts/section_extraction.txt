The requirements section contains information about what candidates must have, should have, or are expected to bring to the role. This includes:
- Required skills, experience, education, or certifications
- Preferred or "nice to have" qualifications
- Language requirements
- Technical proficiencies
- Soft skills or personal attributes

This section may appear under various headings in English or German, such as "Requirements", "Qualifications", "What you bring", "Your profile", "Ihr Profil", "Anforderungen", or similar phrasing. Focus on the semantic meaning rather than exact wording.

Extract the complete text from where the requirements section begins until it ends (usually before sections describing what the company offers, benefits, application instructions, or company background).

If there are multiple requirement-related sections (e.g., "Must have" AND "Nice to have"), include ALL of them.

Do NOT include:
- Job description/responsibilities (what you will do in the role)
- Company description (about the organization)
- Benefits/perks (what the company offers)
- Application instructions
- Contact information

Return ONLY the extracted requirements text. If no requirements section can be identified, return "NO_REQUIREMENTS_SECTION_FOUND".
